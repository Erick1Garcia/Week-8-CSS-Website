History of this tournament

Of the 8 teams remaining only 4 have won it, in the modern era. With Real Madrid winning 6 since the 2000's. Bayern Munich with 3 and both Chelsea and Liverpool with 2.
The record holder with the most goals scored in this tournament is Cristiano Ronaldo with 141 goals, with Messi coming in second with 125 goals.
During the 5 past years the average viewership of the Champions League Final has been 320 million views globally.
